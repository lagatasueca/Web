EMMA LJUNGQVIST

About

I'm a design student at Beckmans College of Design in Stockholm, currently pursuing my bachelor in Fine Art in Visual Communication.

My work moves across graphic identity, print, digital, and motion, with a strong interest in how design can interact with technology.

I like exploring typography, image-making, animation, and interactive design, often combining digital tools with more hands-on, analogue processes.

I'm especially drawn to 3D, creative coding, and experimental workflows where design becomes something you can experience.

I work in a concept-driven way, developing ideas both independently and together with others. I'm interested in creating work that feels thoughtful and intuitive, balancing expression with structure and strategy.
